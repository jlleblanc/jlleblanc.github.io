A Flash-based mp3 player

I've integrated an mp3 player originally written by Jeroen Wijering ( http://www.jeroenwijering.com/ ) into a Joomla component. Click here to downoload it. You can use the backend to customize your own playlist as well as adjust a few of the parameters. This development has been in conjunction with a larger project commissioned by Siobhan Ramsey: http://www.sandboxeducation.co.uk/.
